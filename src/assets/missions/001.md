# Mission // #001

## A little sightseeing

*“This job will be easy. In and out with no real threats except wet socks. Your main problem will be finding ways to entertain yourselves. - Tobias Goodluck, Union DOJ/HR Administrator”*  


# Goals

- Make landfall on *Doppler-14*.
- Escort the skeleton crew to the relay node station *"sightseeing"*.
- Repair the relay node station, and establish a new connection to the omninet.

# Stakes

- The war on *Doppler-14* 30 years ago has left the planet inhospitable, and unstable.
- Reports have come in of vessels in the area around the *Doppler-14* system has gone missing

---

> Since the war on *Doppler-14* the area around the system has been a deadzone for all omninet trafic. It has cause major issues for the unions expansion around the *Sierra Madre Line*. The enviorment and terrain on the planet has been reported as highly dangerous with raging storms and constant rain since the war. The skeleton crew consisting of *Raynold O'niel* and *Karin Nicol* has been placed in your care. Your pilot *Devon Orm* is in training and will need leanancy. You will be compensated extra for this. Aid with the landfall, protect the crew from external factors, and reestablish the omninet connection.
