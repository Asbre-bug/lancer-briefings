During an attack on the Teardrop hotel in Fission city orchestrated by the Badgers, another large scale attack occurred at the Fission Power Plant causing a large explosion, the death of 20 workers and causing a major blackout in Fission causing millions in damage. It is believed that these events are connected as members of the Badgers Terrorist group was spotted extracting other members of their group at the entrance of the Teardrop hotel after a brutal shootout with the Tacex.
